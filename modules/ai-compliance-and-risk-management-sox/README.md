## About
This module examines how AI intersects with SOX compliance. This module discusses how to strengthen AI controls, where it introduces risk, and how to design automated systems that satisfy SOX audit standards.

## Module Outcomes
- Identify where AI can augment SOX workflows safely
- Design audit trails that external auditors will accept
- Build validation patterns that prevent hallucinations and ensure reproducibility
- Apply the "deterministic first, AI second" principle to real workflows
