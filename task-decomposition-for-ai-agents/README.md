## About

This module focuses on the art of breaking down large tasks for an AI. After reviewing examples of good and counter-intuitive decompositions, participants will work in a group to tackle a complex problem and justify their team's approach.

## Module Objectives
- Explain why task decomposition for AI agents often differs from logical, human-centric decomposition.
- Analyze examples of effective and ineffective AI task decomposition to identify key principles
