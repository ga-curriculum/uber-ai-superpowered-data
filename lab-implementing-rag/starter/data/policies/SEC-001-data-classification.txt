SEC-001: Data Classification Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2024

1. Classification Levels

Public: Information that can be freely shared.
Internal: Information for employees only.
Confidential: Sensitive business information requiring protection.
Restricted: Highly sensitive data (PII, financial records, trade secrets).

2. Handling Confidential Data
- Encrypt at rest and in transit
- Access on need-to-know basis only
- Log all access attempts
- Report breaches within 24 hours to security@company.com
