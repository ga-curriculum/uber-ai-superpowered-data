PAY-002: Expense Approval Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Purpose
This policy establishes approval thresholds for business expenses.

2. Single Approval (Under $10,000)
Expenses under $10,000 require approval from one of:
- Direct manager
- Department head

3. Dual Approval ($10,000 and above)
Expenses of $10,000 or more require approval from BOTH:
- Department head
- Finance officer

4. Documentation Requirements
All expenses must include:
- Itemized receipt
- Business justification
- Project code (if applicable)
