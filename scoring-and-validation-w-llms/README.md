## About

This module addresses the critical challenge: "How do we know if the LLM's output is good?" It will introduce frameworks and techniques for scoring and validating the quality and alignment of LLM-generated content.

## Module Outcomes
- Implement programmatic checks to score LLM outputs based on objective criteria (e.g., faithfulness to source data, structured formatting).
- Develop a rubric for the human evaluation of LLM outputs, focusing on qualitative aspects like usefulness, tone, and audience alignment.
- Combine programmatic scores and human feedback to form a holistic judgment on an AI system's production-readiness.
